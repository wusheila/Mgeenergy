Corporate Leadership

MGE Energy Board of Directors is committed to serving the interests of our shareholders, customers and employees. Board members, chosen from the community, provide oversight of performance, strategic planning, business initiatives, major capital projects and budget matters. Directors are elected to serve three-year terms.
